CHATBOT

The CLINT Socratic Bot, is a tool designed to make the learning more engaging and reflective, considering critical thinking as the main way to fight disinformation. This tool, instead of giving you the final response in the first place, it guides you through a short dialogue of questions and answers, helping you reflect about the topic you asked about.Created with a custom build knowledge base, this bot turns every interaction into an opportunity to explore ideas and deepen understanding. Discover how dialogue can foster critical thinking and prepare yourself towards disinformation
